How to view on Google My Maps

  1. 1 Fill in the form above and click Find Places & Download KML. Your browser will download a .kml file.
  2. 2 Open google.com/maps/d and click + Create a new map.
  3. 3 In the left panel click Import and upload the downloaded .kml file. Two layers appear: Route and Places.
  4. 4 Click Share → Anyone with the link can view to make the map public and copy the share link.
KML contents: a driving route line plus colour-coded markers — green = open now, red = closed, yellow = hours unknown. Each marker links directly to Google Maps.
